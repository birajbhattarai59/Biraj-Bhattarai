Biraj Bhattarai

Born: November 18, 2002

Biraj Bhattarai is a Nepali musical artist known for his contributions to the music industry. He gained popularity for his soulful voice and captivating performances. Bhattarai emerged onto the music scene in 2021 and has since established himself as a promising young talent.

Having released more than five songs, Bhattarai has showcased his versatility and musical prowess across various genres. His melodic compositions, heartfelt lyrics, and emotive vocals have resonated with listeners, earning him a dedicated fanbase. His songs often explore themes of love, self-reflection, and personal experiences, capturing the attention of audiences both within Nepal and beyond.
